Evolution of a Role

From Audience to Author

I initially joined CicekliAI simply to learn. I sat in the audience, attending training sessions to understand the fundamentals of artificial intelligence. But I soon realized that the deepest form of learning comes from synthesizing and sharing knowledge with others.

Dec 2023 – Sep 2025

Media Team Manager

My transition into leadership began with communication. Managing the media team required me to translate the community's highly technical activities into engaging, social media content.

  • Directed the community's social media strategy and brand presence.
  • Promoted community initiatives, workshops, and competition milestones to drive engagement.
Sep 2025 – July 2026

AI Research Team Manager

As the AI environment exploded with new models and breakthroughs, the community needed a dedicated structure to keep pace. I stepped up to lead the AI Research Team. My role shifted from communication to continuous research.

I was responsible for monitoring global AI developments, analyzing emerging technologies, and distilling that research into weekly content.

Consistency & Output

Produced weekly research content for 7 consecutive months.
