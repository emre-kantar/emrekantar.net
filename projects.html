Prominent Projects

Explore my most significant projects that showcase my skills in mechanical design, electronics, and problem-solving. Each project represents my efforts toward quality, innovation, and practical impact.

Portable Battery System in Electric Vehicles

EnergyCore

Designed and developed a portable battery system aimed at extending the driving range of electric vehicles in emergency situations. The system emphasizes efficient energy delivery, user-friendly operation, and rapid deployment to provide reliable support when vehicle charging is unavailable.

Agricultural Vehicle for Seeding and Irrigation

AGROBOT

Designed and developed a multifunctional agricultural vehicle capable of performing both seeding and irrigation tasks. The project focuses on improving efficiency and precision in farming operations through practical mechanical design and system integration.

Melodic Generation from Galaxy Images

Cosmic Acoustics

Developed a software that converts pixel colors and brightness from galaxy images into melodies using the golden ratio and fractal geometry. Combining AI, mathematics, and astronomy, it explores the creative connection between art and science through music.

Design of an Autonomous Flying Car System

FlyThrough

Focused on integrating sensor technologies with optimized algorithms and utilizing simulation-based testing to evaluate and improve the performance of an autonomous flying car system. The work aims to enhance reliability, control, and safety for future urban air mobility.
